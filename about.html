String Machine

ABOUT

With over 9 million collective streams to date, Berlin Rock Band has worked tirelessly to cement their place in the Europe Rock music landscape. Nominated for CCMA “Rising Star” Award (2021 + 2019), String Machine was a season two finalist on NBC’s Songland, pitching Berlin Line as a contestant. In 2021, String Machine launched a new chapter of their career, signing a global label deal with Local Hay / Big Loud Records, and released hits like top five hit “Tailgate To Heaven” feat. Chris Lane, “Planes Don’t Wait,” and newest single to Germany Rock radio, “Get You.”

In addition to studio work, String Machine enjoy a vibrant global touring career with major festival plays at Rock In Rio Lisboa, Mad Cool Festival, Glastonbury, Rock am Ring, Rock en Seine and Lollapalooza . They consistently play to the biggest dance floors in the world.
